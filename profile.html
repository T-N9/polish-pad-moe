About LVNDR Nails

Welcome to LVNDR Nails! I'm Hannah; lead nail tech & owner of LVNDR Nails based in the heart of Glasgow City Centre! I am based in the beautiful FX Nail Artistry Studio in the most central location of them all; next to central station! I am a fully qualified & trained acrylic and gel nail technician. I use only the highest quality of products for my clients so that your nails look fierce 24/7! I specialise in creative nail pieces, so if you love that then you've come to the right place! If a plain colour is more your thing, I still cover that to! If you have any questions on art, appointments or anything else don't hesitate to message me directly on my instagram (@lvndr_nails) and I can help you with any query.

* Please note, I have a 48 hour cancellation period regarding appointments & a deposit is required upon each booking. Please make yourself aware of this before booking in, it can be found on my instagram highlights entitled "POLICIES".
